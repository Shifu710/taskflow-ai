# Demo Script

1. Open `/`.
2. Click **Try Guest Demo**.
3. Confirm the demo workspace opens.
4. Open **Workflows**.
5. Open **Competitor Research & Outreach Agent**.
6. Click **Run**.
7. Watch the live run page show planner and tool steps.
8. Approve the approval gate.
9. Confirm reviewer and formatter complete.
10. Confirm the final structured report appears.
11. Open **Usage** and confirm model/tool calls and credits.
12. Replay the run.
13. Open `/technical-review`.
